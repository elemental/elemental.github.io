DiagonalScale¶

Performs either \(X := \mbox{op}(D) X\) or \(X := X \mbox{op}(D)\), where \(op(D)\) equals \(D=D^T\), or \(D^H=\bar D\), where \(D = \mbox{diag}(d)\) and \(d\) is a column vector.
