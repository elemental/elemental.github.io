Affine Quadratic Programs¶

\[\begin{split}\min_{x,s} & \{\; \frac{1}{2} x^T Q x + c^T x \; | \; A x = b \;\wedge\; G x + s = h,\; s \ge 0 \;\}, \\ \max_{y,z} & \{\; -\frac{1}{2} r^T Q^\dagger r - b^T y - h^T z \; | \; r = A^T y + G^T z + c \in \text{range}(Q) \;\wedge\; z \ge 0 \;\}\end{split}\]
