Hadamard¶

Note

This is not a standard BLAS routine, but it is BLAS-like.

The Hadamard product of two \(m \times n\) matrices \(A\) and \(B\) is given entrywise by \(\alpha_{i,j} \beta_{i,j}\) and denoted by \(C = A \circ B\).
