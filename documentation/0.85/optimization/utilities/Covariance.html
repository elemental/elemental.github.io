Covariance¶

The following routines compute the covariance matrix

\[S := \frac{1}{n-1} \sum_{j=0}^{n-1} (d_j-\bar d) (d_j-\bar d)^H,\]

where \(\bar d\) is the average of the \(n\) observations (the columns of \(D\)).
