Extended Kahan¶

The upper-triangular matrix \(A = S R\), where \(S=\text{diag}(1,\zeta,...,\zeta^{3 2^k-1})\), and

\[\begin{split}R = \begin{pmatrix} I & -\phi H_k & 0 \\ 0 & I & \phi H_k \\ 0 & 0 & I \end{pmatrix}.\end{split}\]
